Presentation guidelines

  • Time slots for oral presentations consist of 15 minutes for the talk, followed by 5 minutes for questions and discussion. You are welcome to use your own laptop for the presentation. Alternatively, you can send it in PowerPoint or PDF format to ecrfb26@gmail.com.

  • Please make sure your poster does not exceed a maximum of 120 x 150 cm and is in portrait form.

Accommodation, Travel, and Reimbursement

This conference has no registration fee. Participants are responsible for arranging and paying for their own travel and accommodation. For accommodation, we have reserved a number of rooms at Hotel Melchior for the nights of June 30 to July 3. Booking details and instructions will be sent by email upon acceptance of your talk or poster.

The standard arrangement is a shared double room. You may indicate a preferred roommate in the Registration form. A limited number of single rooms is available for participants with special requirements. All rooms include breakfast.

The accommodation costs for rooms at Hotel Melchior can be fully reimbursed. Further information about the reimbursement procedure will be provided after the event.

You are free to choose another hotel; however, reimbursement is only possible for the suggested one with which the university has an agreement. Please note that the suggested accommodation is the most conveniently located for reaching the venue.
