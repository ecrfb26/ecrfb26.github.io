Program

The conference program features 26 talks (6 invited and 20 contributed) organized into four thematic blocks, highlighting both theoretical and experimental advances in Flat Band Physics. Invited talks are 50 minutes long, starting with a 20-minute pedagogical introduction, followed by a 20-minute presentation of the speaker’s own work, and 10 minutes for questions. Contributed talks are 20 minutes long, with 15 minutes for presentation and 5 minutes for discussion. All participants will have the opportunity to present their research during a poster session at the end of the first day.
