Early Career Researchers on Flat Bands 2026

July 1–3 2026, JMU Würzburg, Germany

Flat band systems have emerged as one of the most exciting topics in condensed matter physics. From strongly correlated electronic phases to topological phenomena and unconventional superconductivity, flat bands provide a powerful platform for exploring interaction-driven quantum states.

This workshop will focus on recent theoretical and experimental advances in flat band physics from the perspective of early-career researchers working in the field.

Topics:

  • Novel quantum phases and emergent phenomena in flat band systems
  • Kagome and other frustrated lattice systems
  • Moiré heterostructures and twisted multilayers
  • Interplay of quantum geometry, topology, and electron correlations
